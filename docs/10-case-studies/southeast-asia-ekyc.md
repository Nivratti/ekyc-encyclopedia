# Southeast Asia: Emerging Markets eKYC

## Overview

SE Asia's diverse eKYC landscape — rapid digital adoption, mobile-first populations, varied regulatory maturity, and financial inclusion as a driver.

---

## Regional Overview

| Country | Key ID | eKYC Maturity | Notable |
|---------|--------|--------------|---------|
| **Singapore** | NRIC + Singpass | Advanced | MyInfo zero-document eKYC |
| **Indonesia** | e-KTP | Growing | Dukcapil database verification |
| **Philippines** | PhilSys (new) | Emerging | National ID modeled on Aadhaar |
| **Thailand** | Thai ID card | Growing | NDID framework |
| **Vietnam** | CCCD (chip-based) | Emerging | New chip-based national ID |
| **Malaysia** | MyKad | Moderate | Chip-based smart ID card |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - **Mobile-first** populations — mobile eKYC adoption is natural
    - **Singapore** is the regional leader — Singpass/MyInfo is the gold standard
    - **Financial inclusion** drives eKYC adoption — serving unbanked populations
    - **National digital ID programs** (PhilSys, Vietnam CCCD) are modernizing the landscape
