# Gaming & Age Verification

## Overview

Age verification for gaming, gambling, alcohol, and adult content — minimal data collection, privacy-focused, high conversion requirements.

---

## Age Verification Methods

| Method | Assurance | Friction | Privacy |
|--------|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Self-declaration** | None | Zero | High |
| **Credit card check** | Low | Low | Medium |
| **ID document scan** | High | Medium | Low (full ID captured) |
| **Database age check** | High | Low | Medium (name + DOB only) |
| **Digital credential** | High | Low | High (selective disclosure: age > 18 only) |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - **Selective disclosure** (from EUDI Wallet or VCs) is the ideal solution — prove age without revealing identity
    - Until wallets are widespread, **database age checks** offer best balance of assurance and privacy
    - EU and UK regulations increasingly mandate **robust age verification** for online platforms
