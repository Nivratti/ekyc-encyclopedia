# Government Digital Services

## Overview

Government-scale digital identity and eKYC — serving entire populations with inclusive, multi-channel, high-assurance verification for public services.

---

## Government eKYC Scale

| Country | System | Scale |
|---------|--------|-------|
| **India** | Aadhaar | 1.4B identities, 100M+ eKYC/month |
| **Estonia** | eID | 1.3M citizens, 99% digital government |
| **Singapore** | Singpass | 4.5M users, 40M+ annual transactions |
| **UAE** | UAE Pass | 5M+ users |
| **UK** | GOV.UK Verify → One Login | 60M+ population target |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - Government eKYC must be **universal** — serve everyone including elderly, disabled, low-literacy
    - **Multi-channel** (app, web, agent, branch) is mandatory for inclusion
    - **India's Aadhaar** is the world's largest — a reference architecture for population-scale eKYC
    - Government systems become **platform infrastructure** — private sector builds on top
