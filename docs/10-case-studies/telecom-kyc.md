# Telecom KYC

## Overview

Telecom KYC for SIM registration — high-volume, low-cost, often mandated by government regulation to combat terrorism and fraud.

---

## Telecom KYC Characteristics

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Volume** | Millions of SIM registrations per month (large operators) |
| **Cost sensitivity** | Must be < $0.50 per verification |
| **Regulatory driver** | Government SIM registration mandates (India, Nigeria, UAE, etc.) |
| **Assurance level** | Lower than banking — basic identity verification |
| **Method** | Aadhaar OTP (India), document + selfie, agent-assisted |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - Telecom is the **highest-volume** eKYC use case — millions per month per operator
    - **Cost** is the primary driver — must be significantly cheaper than banking eKYC
    - **Government mandates** (SIM registration laws) drive adoption
    - India's telecom eKYC (Aadhaar OTP) is the largest implementation globally
