## Vendor Landscape

|--------|---------------|-------------|

### Cloud AI Services

| Service | Approach | Best For |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Google Document AI** | Pre-trained + custom models | Flexible, scalable |
| **AWS Textract** | Table and form extraction | AWS-native workflows |
| **Azure Form Recognizer** | Pre-built and custom models | Enterprise Azure shops |
| **Google Cloud Vision OCR** | General-purpose OCR | Basic text extraction |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - **Regula** has the deepest document forensics and widest coverage (14,000+ types)
    - **Microblink** is the leader in on-device document SDK
    - **Full-stack vendors** (Jumio, Onfido, Veriff) combine document + face verification
    - **Cloud AI services** are flexible but lack document-specific forensics and liveness
    - Choice depends on: coverage needs, on-device vs server, forensic depth, budget
