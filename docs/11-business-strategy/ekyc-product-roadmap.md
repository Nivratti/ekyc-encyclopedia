## Typical Product Evolution

| Stage | Capabilities | Timeline |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **MVP** | Document OCR + face match + basic liveness | Month 1-6 |
| **V1** | + document forensics, sanctions screening, manual review | Month 6-12 |
| **V2** | + advanced liveness, NFC, multi-country support | Year 1-2 |
| **V3** | + orchestration, analytics, risk scoring | Year 2-3 |
| **Platform** | + credential verification, continuous monitoring, fraud graph | Year 3+ |

## Current Priority Features (2025-2026)

| Feature | Priority | Driver |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **Deepfake/injection prevention** | Critical | AI fraud increasing exponentially |
| **EUDI Wallet credential acceptance** | High | EU mandate 2026-2027 |
| **NFC document reading** | High | Highest assurance verification |
| **Fairness/bias testing** | High | EU AI Act requirement |
| **Orchestration** | Medium | Multi-vendor market trend |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - eKYC products evolve from **point solution** (document + face) to **platform** (full compliance)
    - **Deepfake/injection defense** is the most urgent current feature priority
    - **EUDI Wallet** acceptance is a must-have by 2026-2027 for EU-serving providers
    - Platform play (KYC + fraud + compliance) is the strategic direction for market leaders
