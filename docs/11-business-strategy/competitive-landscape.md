# Competitive Landscape

## Definition

The eKYC vendor market — major players, positioning, differentiation, and competitive dynamics.

---

## Market Map

| Tier | Players | Characteristics |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **Enterprise leaders** | Jumio, Onfido (Entrust), Veriff, iProov | Global, enterprise sales, broad document coverage, iBeta certified |
| **Regional leaders** | HyperVerge (India/SEA), Shufti Pro (MEA), AuthentiQ | Strong in home region, competitive pricing |
| **Full-stack platforms** | Sumsub, Persona, Alloy | KYC + fraud + compliance in one platform |
| **Specialist** | Regula (documents), iProov (liveness), Socure (identity graph) | Best-in-class for specific capability |
| **Infrastructure** | Microblink (SDK), Daon (biometrics) | Components and SDKs for building eKYC |
| **Cloud AI** | Google, AWS, Azure | General OCR/vision APIs, not eKYC-specific |

## Competitive Dimensions

| Dimension | What It Means |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Document coverage** | Number of document types × countries supported |
| **Accuracy** | NIST FRVT ranking, iBeta certification level |
| **Speed** | Time to verify, STP rate |
| **Price** | Per-verification cost at volume |
| **Integration** | SDK quality, API design, time to integrate |
| **Compliance** | Regulatory certifications, audit support |
| **UX** | Conversion rate, user experience quality |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - Market is **fragmenting into tiers** — enterprise, regional, platform, specialist
    - **No single vendor dominates** — banks often use 2-3 vendors via orchestration
    - **Document coverage** and **accuracy** (NIST/iBeta) are the primary enterprise differentiators
    - **Pricing pressure** is increasing — commoditization of basic verification
    - **Platform play** (Sumsub, Persona, Alloy) is the emerging strategy — combine KYC + fraud + compliance
