# Partner Ecosystem

## Definition

The network of partners that eKYC providers work with — data providers, system integrators, technology partners, and channel partners.

---

## Partner Types

| Partner Type | Examples | Value |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| **Data providers** | World-Check, Dow Jones, LexisNexis | Sanctions, PEP, adverse media data |
| **Database verification** | UIDAI, NSDL, credit bureaus, DVS | Government identity verification APIs |
| **System integrators** | Deloitte, Accenture, TCS | Enterprise implementation, compliance consulting |
| **Technology partners** | AWS, Azure, GCP | Cloud infrastructure, AI services |
| **Channel partners** | Core banking vendors (Temenos, Finastra) | Pre-integrated into banking platforms |
| **Industry bodies** | FIDO Alliance, OpenID Foundation, DIF | Standards participation, credibility |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - **Data partnerships** (sanctions, PEP lists) are critical — no eKYC works without screening data
    - **Core banking integrations** (Temenos, Finastra) provide distribution to banks
    - **System integrators** drive enterprise adoption — they recommend and implement eKYC vendors
    - Building a partner ecosystem is **as important as building the product**
