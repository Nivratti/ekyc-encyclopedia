# Estonia e-Residency

## Definition

**Estonia e-Residency** is the world's first government-issued digital identity for non-residents, enabling anyone globally to establish an EU-based business and access Estonian digital services using a secure digital ID.

---

## Key Features

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Launched** | 2014 — first country to offer digital identity to non-residents |
| **e-Residents** | 100,000+ from 170+ countries |
| **Digital ID card** | Smart card with PKI certificates for authentication + digital signatures |
| **X-Road** | Secure data exchange layer connecting government databases |
| **99% digital** | Nearly all government services available online |
| **Digital signatures** | Legally binding, EU-recognized under eIDAS |

## Estonia's Digital Infrastructure

| Component | What It Does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **eID / Mobile-ID / Smart-ID** | Authentication + digital signatures for citizens |
| **X-Road** | Secure inter-institutional data exchange (no central database) |
| **KSI Blockchain** | Integrity verification for government records |
| **e-Business Register** | Company registration in 15 minutes online |
| **e-Tax** | Tax filing in 3 minutes |

---

## Key Takeaways

!!! success "Summary"
    - Estonia is the **world's most advanced digital government** — 99% of services online
    - **No central database** — X-Road connects distributed databases with end-to-end encryption
    - **e-Residency** proves digital identity can transcend physical borders
    - The model demonstrates what **fully digital eKYC** looks like in a mature ecosystem
    - Estonia's approach influenced **eIDAS 2.0** and EUDI Wallet design
